The Year 2020

https://arrowteam.net/
April/2020

In the year 2020, I became the team manager for the third year and the fourth competition. That year, we had quite a good time in our timeplan, as we were able to design a full electric car with suspension (for the first time) and only weigh 130kg.
The 2020 project was sponsored by Brightskies company along with Shell Eco-Marathon.

I started mastering python and TensorFlow that year. As I had my graduation project in the field of machine learning, I also had a great experience with model based design using Simulink in MATLAB, and you will get to know more below.
Starting first with my work in the battery management systems,
I used MATLAB in building a 18650_Cell model (Lithium-ion Cell) and inherited all its dynamics to further build a management system that determins the state of charge of the cell. Using the algorithm of the SOC (Coulomb Counting) in the 2020 Electric Car.
I also built the Nvidia End-to-End self-driving algorithm that predicts the steering angle of the car based on the road features seen from a computer vision model.
